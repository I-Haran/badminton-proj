About Us

Established in 2018 in Iyyappanthangal, MSR Sports Academy offers top-quality badminton coaching. Conveniently located near Ramachandra Hospital, behind Prince Highlands and Dakshin Apartment, it is easily accessible for Chennai residents. The vibrant surroundings enhance the energetic atmosphere, making it an ideal choice for aspiring players.
